Winter Birding in Cheorwon

Published on Jan 14, 2026 by Andy

Cheorwon represents one of the most significant wintering grounds for cranes in East Asia. Located near the DMZ, this area provides a pristine habitat for these majestic birds.


Why Cheorwon?

Every winter, thousands of White-naped Cranes and Red-crowned Cranes migrate to this area. The vast rice paddies provide ample food, while the restrictions of the civilian control line offer them safety.


Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit is from December to February. Early morning is particularly magical as you can see the cranes flying out from their roosting sites against the sunrise.
